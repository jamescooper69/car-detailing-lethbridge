Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book my detailing service?

We recommend booking at least 3-5 days in advance, especially during peak seasons. For same-week appointments, please call us directly to check availability.

What forms of payment do you accept?

We accept only e-transfers and cash for now. Payment is due upon completion of service.

What is your cancellation policy?

We require 24 hours notice for cancellations. Cancellations with less than 24 hours notice may be subject to a cancellation fee of 10% of the service price.
