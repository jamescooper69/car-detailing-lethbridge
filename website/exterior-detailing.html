Exterior Detailing

Premium Exterior Detailing

Restore your vehicle's shine with our professional exterior detailing service. We bring the showroom finish to your doorstep in Lethbridge.

Our exterior detailing service is designed to clean, correct, and protect your vehicle's paint, wheels, and trim, ensuring a lasting shine that turns heads.

Book This Service

What's Included

Hand Wash

Gentle two-bucket hand wash to safely remove dirt and contaminants without scratching the paint.

Clay Bar Treatment

Clay bar decontamination to remove embedded particles and provide a smooth surface.

Paint Correction

Machine polishing to remove light scratches, swirl marks, and oxidation from the paint surface.

Wheel & Tire Detailing

Deep cleaning of wheels and tires, including dressing for a long-lasting shine.

Paint Protection

Application of premium wax or sealant to protect the paint and enhance gloss.

Exterior Glass Cleaning

Streak-free cleaning of all exterior glass surfaces for optimal visibility.
