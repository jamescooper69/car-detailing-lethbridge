Frequently Asked Questions

Do you service areas outside of Lethbridge?

While our primary service area is Lethbridge, we absolutely serve several nearby towns as well, including Raymond, Taber, Picture Butte, Coalhurst, and Coaldale, in addition to Lethbridge itself.

Do I need to provide water or electricity for your mobile service?

Yes, we kindly ask that you provide access to a basic water supply and a standard electrical outlet. This allows us to operate our equipment efficiently and deliver the best results for your detailing service.

How much space do you need to perform your services?

We typically need about 15-20 feet of space around your vehicle to work efficiently. This can be in a driveway, parking lot, or street parking where permitted.

What if the weather is bad on my appointment day?

If weather conditions are not suitable for detailing (heavy rain, snow, or extreme temperatures), we'll contact you to reschedule your appointment at no additional charge.
