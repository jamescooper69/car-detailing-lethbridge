About Us

Our Story

Bridge Auto Studio was founded with a simple mission: to provide the residents of Lethbridge with premium mobile car detailing services that exceed expectations.

What started as a passion for pristine vehicles has grown into Lethbridge's most trusted mobile detailing service. We take pride in our attention to detail and commitment to customer satisfaction.

Our team consists of certified detailing professionals who are trained in the latest techniques and use only premium products to ensure your vehicle receives the care it deserves.

A Local Business With a Clear Mission

Founded in Lethbridge, Bridge Auto Studio started with one goal: to make professional-grade detailing accessible and stress-free. From sedans to SUVs, we’ve built a reputation for delivering showroom results without the dealership markup — and always with a personal touch.
